If you've worked with us before, you probably knew us as Galvez Stone. After more than 20 years quarrying Tennessee flagstone, slabs, and treads out of our Graysville property, we've rebranded to Deer Creek Stone.

We want to be upfront about what that means — and, just as important, what it doesn't.

## What's changed

The name, and the look that goes with it. Galvez Stone is now Deer Creek Stone, with a new logo and a refreshed website. That's it. If you're searching for us and only remember "Galvez Stone," you're in the right place — same business, same property, same phone numbers.

## What hasn't changed

Everything that actually matters to the people we work with:

- **Same quarry.** We're still quarrying out of the same Graysville, TN property we always have.
- **Same stone.** The flagstone, slabs, treads, and wall stone you've bought from us before are unchanged.
- **Same direct pricing.** No middleman markup then, none now — you're still buying straight from the quarry.
- **Same people.** The team grading your pallets and answering the phone is the same team it's always been.

## Why rebrand after 20+ years?

Honestly — the Galvez Stone name had run its course, and we wanted a brand that better reflected who we are today: a family-owned Tennessee quarry shipping nationwide to homeowners, landscapers, and builders, with stone for everything from patios and pool coping to fireplaces, benches, and tables. Deer Creek Stone felt like a better fit for where the business has grown.

## Still have questions?

If you're a returning customer and something about this transition is unclear, give us a call — both lines still ring through to the same place. You'll find current numbers on our [contact page](/contact).

We're not going anywhere. We just look a little different.
